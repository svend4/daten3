# TravelHub Affiliate Program Agreement

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to the TravelHub Affiliate Program ("Program"). This Agreement governs your participation in the Program.

## 2. Program Overview

The TravelHub Affiliate Program allows you to earn commissions by referring customers to TravelHub through your unique referral link.

### 2.1 Commission Structure

- **Level 1 (Direct Referrals):** 3% of booking value
- **Level 2 (Sub-Affiliates):** 1.5% of booking value
- **Level 3 (Third Level):** 0.5% of booking value

### 2.2 Cookie Duration

Referral cookies are valid for 30 days from the first click.

## 3. Eligibility

To participate in the Program, you must:
- Be at least 18 years old
- Have a valid email address
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
- Not engage in fraudulent or unethical practices

## 4. Payment Terms

### 4.1 Minimum Payout

The minimum payout threshold is $50 USD.

### 4.2 Payment Schedule

Payments are processed within 5 business days of approval.

### 4.3 Commission Hold Period

Commissions are held for 30 days to allow for refunds and cancellations.

### 4.4 Payment Methods

We support the following payment methods:
- PayPal
- Bank Transfer
