
## Caching Strategy

- Flight searches: 1 hour
- Hotel searches: 2 hours
- Hotel details: 24 hours
- Location autocomplete: 7 days

## Rate Limiting

- Strict: 5 req/min (resource-intensive)
- Moderate: 10 req/min (default API)
- Lenient: 30 req/min (light operations)
- Very Lenient: 100 req/min (static data)

## Monitoring

Logs are stored in `logs/` directory:
- `combined.log` - All logs
- `error.log` - Errors only
