## Performance

### Throughput

- **Embedding Generation**: ~50 docs/sec (CPU), ~200 docs/sec (GPU)
- **Search Latency**: <100ms for 100K documents
- **Batch Size**: 32 documents optimal

### Resource Usage

**CPU Mode**:
- Memory: ~4GB
- CPU: 2-4 cores

**GPU Mode**:
- Memory: ~8GB GPU RAM
- Speed: 4x faster than CPU
