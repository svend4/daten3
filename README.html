Sign up for your partner ID:

http://www.ian.com/ian/signup/signup.jsp

NOTE: When signing up for your partner account, be sure to enter start.php after your URL (domain). Therefor if you domain was http://www.mytravelsite.com, you would put: http://www.mytravelsite.com/start.php

This is so that the home botton as determined on the affiliate home page directs back to your site. If you just use your domain name, then the site will reopen frames inside the frames which you do not want.

Manual Setup

Open common.php is your text editor and change the variables.

Installer

Upload your site and set permissions on common.php to 666

Open your website in your browser and run http://www.yourdomain.com/setup and fill out the form. If you use the installer you may wish to delete the setup folder after your site is installed.

 

Your site is now installed!
